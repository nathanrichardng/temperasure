Temperasure

Easily manage your laboratory's temperature readings in real time!

Getting started:

  1. Sign up for Temperasure using the button on the top right of the nav bar.
  2. Click "Locations" (on the left-hand side of the navbar) and add the locations where you have placed your thermometers. For example, the fridge in the front. Or the incubator in the back of the lab.
  3. Once you have some locations added, click one to view a history of it's temperature recordings. From here you can add new recordings, or choose which dates you would like to review.

Today's log:

Click "Today's log" to see:

  1. A quick-list of all your locations
  2. The most recent temperature readings from today
  3. Accepted values in green.
    Unacceptible values in red

Collaborate in real time:

  1. Click "Locations"
  2. Find a location you would like to share.
  3. Click "Edit"
  4. Add the user's email address to your list of shared users.

Once a location is shared, any changes made by one user will immediately update for all the other members (assuming they are connected to the internet).
